I am the developer of Diagnoz. I am a passionate software engineer with experience in developing predictive models for healthcare applications. My goal is to create innovative solutions that can help improve patient outcomes and assist healthcare professionals in making informed decisions.

I developed Diagnoz as a tool to help people predict their risk for various diseases based on a set of input features. The tool is powered by advanced machine learning algorithms that have been trained on large datasets to provide accurate predictions.

I hold a post graduate degree in M.Sc Information Technology and have worked on several data-driven projects in the past. I am constantly learning and expanding my skillset to keep up with the latest trends and advancements in the field of data science.

Thank you for using Diagnoz, and please feel free to reach out to me with any questions or feedback.
